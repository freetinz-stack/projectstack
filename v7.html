No cloud by default

Your data lives
only on your device.

FincWin stores everything in your browser's localStorage. No account needed. No server receives your financial data. The app works completely offline, always.

If you've been burned by data breaches at financial apps, this is the architecture you've been waiting for.

Security features

Three layers of protection
built into every session.

🔐

PIN lock

Your finances are behind a PIN — not a password reset email. Set a 4-6 digit PIN and your data stays locked on shared or lost devices. No account needed to protect it.

🔒

AES-GCM encryption

Choose cloud sync and your data is encrypted with AES-GCM using a passphrase only you know — before it leaves your device. FincWin's servers never receive decrypted data.

📱

QR device pairing

Add a second device by scanning a QR code. No account, no login, no sharing your credentials. Your data transfers encrypted, device to device.
