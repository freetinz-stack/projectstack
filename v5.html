The envelope method

Allocate before you spend.
That changes everything.

Traditional budgets fail because you see the damage after it's done. The envelope method flips that: you assign your income to categories before you spend, so every dollar has a destination.

Step 1

Set your envelopes

Give each spending category a monthly cap. Housing gets $1,500. Groceries gets $400. Dining gets $200. Each category has a limit before the month begins.

Housing
$0 / $1,500
Step 2

Watch the bars fill

Log expenses as you spend. Each entry fills the envelope bar. When Housing hits 70% on day 12, you know three weeks in advance — not at month end.

Housing
$1,050 / $1,500
Step 3

Stay in or decide to exceed

When the bar approaches the cap, you make an intentional choice — not an accidental one. Stay under, adjust the cap, or decide it's worth going over. Your choice. Full information.
