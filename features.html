Budget Envelopes

Spend what you
planned to spend.

The envelope method is the oldest budgeting system on earth — because it works. FincWin digitises it without the faff. Before the month starts, assign an amount to each of 14 categories. As you log expenses, the envelope fills. When it's full, you know.

  • 14 built-in categories: housing, food, auto, telecom, healthcare, entertainment, subscriptions, and more
  • Real-time spend percentage with colour-coded warnings
  • Surplus rollover — see what you underspent last month
  • Projected overspend alerts based on current pace

Know the exact date
you become debt-free.

  • Full amortisation schedule: see every payment's principal vs. interest split
  • Extra payment modelling — drag and drop to see payoff date shift
  • Avalanche and snowball strategy comparison
  • DTI (debt-to-income) ratio indicator shown on dashboard

See the days that
cost you most.

Numbers in a table don't show patterns. A heat-map does. The calendar view lights up the days you spent heavily — so you can spot whether it's every Friday, every payday, or every time a subscription renews.

  • Daily spend intensity colour-coded across the full month
  • Hover any day to see a breakdown of what you spent
  • Navigate backward through months to compare patterns
  • Instant visual for high-spend vs. low-spend periods
