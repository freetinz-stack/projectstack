User Guide · All Features

Everything FincWin does,
explained.

This guide covers every feature with step-by-step instructions. Use the sidebar to jump to any section. All your data stays on your device — no account required.

01 — Getting Started

First launch & setup

When you open FincWin for the first time, a short setup wizard walks you through your income, expenses, loans, savings, PIN, and storage preference. This takes about 2–3 minutes.

How to complete the setup wizard
1
Open FincWin in your browser. The welcome screen appears automatically on first visit.
2
Step 1 — Your name & currency. Enter your first name and select your home currency from the dropdown.
3
Step 2 — Income sources. Add each monthly income stream (salary, freelance, etc.) with a name and amount. Click + Add income for each one.
4
Step 3 — Monthly expenses. Add your recurring bills (rent, phone, subscriptions). Each gets a name, amount, and optional due day.
5
Step 4 — Loans & debts. Add any outstanding loans with balance, interest rate, and minimum payment.
6
Step 5 — Savings goals. Add savings accounts or goals with current balance and monthly contribution.
7
Step 6 — PIN protection. Set a 4–6 digit PIN to protect your data. You can skip this and enable it later in Settings.
8
Step 7 — Storage. Choose where your data lives: browser storage (default), cloud sync, or a local file. You can change this any time in Settings.
9
Click Open Dashboard. Your dashboard is ready with this month pre-populated.
💡
Skip the wizard. On the welcome screen, click "Explore with demo data →" to load a rich sample profile instantly and see every feature in action.

04 — Budgeting & Expenses

Bills, budgets & envelopes

The Bills tab is the heart of FincWin. Expenses are organised into 14 category envelopes. Each envelope has a monthly cap (budget), and a colour-coded bar shows how fast you're spending it.

14 Category Envelopes
Banking, Telecom, Subscriptions, Auto, Utilities, Housing, Food/Meals, Entertainment, Fees, Health, Loan Pmt, Tuition, Savings, Other
Budget Caps
Set a monthly spending limit per category. The bar turns amber at 75% and red when over.
Budget Rollover
Unspent envelope balance carries forward automatically to next month.
Recurring Expenses
Mark any expense as recurring — monthly, weekly, biweekly, quarterly, or yearly.
Multi-Currency Items
Log any expense in a foreign currency. FincWin converts it to your home currency automatically.
Drag-and-Drop Reorder
Drag expense rows to reorder them within a week. Works with mouse or touch.
How to add an expense
1
Go to the Bills tab.
2
Click the + button inside any week slot (Week 1–4), or press the global Quick Add button (bottom-right floating button).
3
Enter the expense name, amount, and optional due day. The category is auto-detected from the name — you can override it.
4
To make it recurring, toggle Recurring and select the frequency.
5
Click Save. The item appears in the chosen week and its category envelope updates immediately.
How to mark an expense as paid
1
Click the checkbox or the expense row's paid toggle. The row dims to show it's settled.
2
To mark all items in a week at once, click the ✓ All button at the top of the week slot.
💡
Unpaid items show in full colour; paid items are dimmed. The health score improves as you clear bills.
How to set a budget cap for a category
1
On the Bills tab, click the Budget button (or the pencil icon next to any envelope name).
2
The Bulk Budget modal opens showing all 14 categories. Enter a cap for each one you want to limit.
3
Click Save. Envelopes now display a spend bar relative to your cap.
How to delete an expense
1
Click the expense row to open it, then click the Delete (trash) button.
2
For bulk deletion: click the Select icon on the week header to enter multi-select mode. Check items, then click Delete Selected.
How to edit an expense amount inline
1
Click directly on the amount number in any expense row. It becomes an editable field.
2
Type the new amount and press Enter or click away to save.

06 — Loans & Debt

Paying down debt

The Loans tab tracks every debt — student loans, car loans, mortgages, credit cards. It calculates your exact payoff date and shows the impact of extra payments.

Amortisation Schedule
Full month-by-month breakdown of principal, interest, and remaining balance.
Payoff Date Calculator
See the exact month your loan reaches zero with your current payment.
Extra Payment Simulator
Enter an extra monthly amount and see how many months and dollars in interest you save.
DTI Indicator
Live Debt-to-Income ratio badge. Below 36% is healthy; above 43% is a warning.
Avalanche vs Snowball
Switch paydown strategy: Avalanche (highest APR first) or Snowball (smallest balance first).
Debt-Free Celebration
Confetti animation and a badge when a loan reaches £0.
How to add a loan
1
Go to the Loans tab and click + Add Loan.
2
Enter: Loan name, current balance, original amount, interest rate (APR %), and minimum monthly payment.
3
Click Save. The payoff date and amortisation schedule calculate instantly.
How to simulate extra payments
1
Open a loan by clicking its row on the Loans tab.
2
In the loan detail panel, find the Extra Payment field and enter an additional monthly amount.
3
The payoff date and total interest update in real time to show how much you save.
💡
Even $50/month extra on a typical loan can cut years off your payoff date. Use the simulator to find your sweet spot.
How to switch between Avalanche and Snowball strategy
1
On the Loans tab, find the Strategy toggle at the top.
2
Click Avalanche to prioritise highest-interest loans first (saves most money).
3
Click Snowball to prioritise smallest-balance loans first (gives fastest psychological wins).

09 — Bulk Add & Bank Import

Adding data in bulk

Bulk Add lets you add many expenses, loans, or savings at once. Bank Import reads a CSV or OFX file directly from your bank and maps transactions into FincWin.

How to use Bulk Add
1
Click the Bulk Add button (found on the Bills tab or via the + menu).
2
Select the tab: Expenses, Loans, or Savings.
3
Enter each item on its own row — name and amount. Press Enter to add another row quickly.
4
Click Save All to import everything at once.
How to import a bank statement (CSV/OFX)
1
Download a statement CSV or OFX file from your bank's website.
2
Go to Settings → Bank Import (or the import icon on the Bills tab).
3
Click Choose File and select your statement file.
4
Use the Column Mapper to match your file's columns to Date, Amount, and Name fields.
5
FincWin detects duplicates and recurring patterns automatically. Review the transactions, assign categories where needed, and click Import Selected.
⚠️
Review before importing. Check the preview list carefully — duplicates are flagged but not automatically excluded.

13 — AI Finance Coach

Your personal finance advisor

FincWin integrates with Claude (Anthropic) and OpenAI so you can ask plain-English questions about your finances. You supply your own API key — no data leaves your device through FincWin's servers.

How to set up the AI coach
1
Go to Settings → AI Coach.
2
Choose your provider: Claude (Anthropic) or OpenAI.
3
Paste your API key into the key field. Keys are stored encrypted on your device.
4
Click the AI Coach button (brain icon) from the Dashboard or Analytics tab to open the chat panel.
5
Ask anything — "Where am I overspending?", "How long to pay off my student loan?", "Should I prioritise savings or debt this month?"
💡
The AI coach receives a summary of your financial data as context — your totals, loan balances, and category spend — but never raw transaction names. Your privacy is protected.
14 — Cloud & File Sync

Your data, everywhere

By default FincWin stores everything locally in your browser. For multi-device access, enable Cloud Sync (Firebase) or link a local JSON file.

How to enable cloud sync
1
Go to Settings → Sync and click Enable Cloud Sync.
2
Enter a passphrase. All data is encrypted with this passphrase before leaving your device. You must remember this — it cannot be recovered.
3
On a second device, open FincWin → Settings → Sync, enable cloud sync, and enter the same passphrase. Your data will sync automatically.
4
Alternatively, use QR Code Sync — one device shows a QR code; scan it with the second device to pair them instantly.
⚠️
Remember your passphrase. If you forget it, your cloud data cannot be decrypted by anyone — including us. Store it safely.
How to resolve a sync conflict
1
If cloud and local data diverge significantly, a Conflict Resolution dialog appears showing both versions side by side.
2
Review the summary of each version (totals, last modified time) and choose Keep Local or Use Cloud.
3
If both versions have identical financial totals, FincWin silently auto-resolves by picking the most recently modified version — no prompt needed.

16 — PIN & Encryption

Protecting your data

FincWin offers two layers of security: a PIN lock (requires entry on app open) and AES-GCM encryption of stored data using your PIN as the key.

How to set a PIN
1
Click the lock icon in the top bar, or go to Settings → Security → Set PIN.
2
Enter a 4–6 digit PIN, confirm it, and click Save.
3
The app will now require this PIN on each new browser session. Within the same session, the PIN is remembered until the auto-lock timeout expires.
How to configure auto-lock timeout
1
Go to Settings → Security → Auto-lock after.
2
Choose a timeout: 30 min, 1 hr, 4 hrs (default), 8 hrs, 1 day, or Never.
3
After this period of inactivity the app locks and asks for your PIN again. Closing the browser tab always locks regardless of timeout.
17 — Backup & Export

Never lose your data

Download a full JSON backup of all your data at any time. Import it back on any device to restore everything exactly.

How to download a backup
1
Go to Settings → Export & Backup.
2
Click Download JSON Backup. A file named fincwin-YYYY-MM-DD.json saves to your downloads.
💡
Downloading a backup also unlocks the Backup Hero badge (+125 XP). Do it regularly.
How to restore from a backup
1
Go to Settings → Import.
2
Click Choose File and select your .json backup file.
3
Confirm the import. All data is replaced with the backup. This cannot be undone.
⚠️
This replaces all current data. Download a fresh backup of your current state first if you want a safety copy before restoring.

23 — FAQ

Common questions

Is my data safe? Does FincWin send it anywhere?

By default, all data lives entirely in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Cloud Sync is opt-in and encrypts all data with your passphrase before it ever leaves your device.

I forgot my PIN — how do I get back in?

If you set a passphrase during PIN setup, use the Passphrase Recovery option on the lock screen. If not, you'll need to clear app data from your browser settings (this erases all FincWin data). This is why regular JSON backups are important.

Can I use FincWin offline?

Yes. FincWin is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with a service worker that caches all assets. After your first visit, it works fully offline. Install it to your home screen for the best experience.

How do I install FincWin to my phone?
1
iOS (Safari): Tap the Share button → Add to Home Screen.
2
Android (Chrome): Tap the three-dot menu → Install app or Add to Home Screen.
3
Desktop (Chrome/Edge): Click the install icon in the address bar.
Will my data transfer if I clear my browser?

Clearing browser storage will erase local FincWin data. Always download a JSON backup before clearing. If you use Cloud Sync, your data is safe in the cloud and will re-sync on next open.
