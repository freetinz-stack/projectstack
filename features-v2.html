01  ·  Budgeting & Expenses
Your money, allocated before you spend it.

14 envelope categories, five recurring frequencies, multi-currency support, and real-time velocity tracking so you always know where you stand.

14 expense categories
Banking, Telecom, Subscriptions, Utilities, Auto, Housing, Food/Meals, Entertainment, Fees, Health, Loan Pmt, Tuition, Savings, Other.
Budget envelopes
Allocate a monthly cap per category. Visual spend bar shows how much of the envelope you've used with colour-coded velocity.
Budget velocity indicator
Colour shifts from green → amber → red as you burn through a cap, giving you instant visual warnings before you overspend.
Envelope collapse/expand
Hide categories with zero spend to reduce clutter and keep your view focused on what matters this month.
Budget rollover
Carry unspent envelope balance forward to the next month so discipline is rewarded, not reset.
Bulk budget editing
Set multiple envelope caps in a single modal — no clicking through each category one by one.
Expense items
Add one-time or recurring expenses to any week slot in the current month.
Recurring frequencies
Monthly, weekly, biweekly, quarterly, and yearly. Quarterly and yearly expenses auto-appear in qualifying months without re-entry.
Multi-currency expenses
Log any item in a different currency. FincWin auto-converts to your home currency using live exchange rates.
Mark paid / unpaid
Toggle payment status per item. Paid items turn green and trigger a small confetti burst.
Bulk mark paid / unpaid all
One tap to settle or unsettle an entire week of expenses without touching each row.
Bulk select & delete
Checkbox multi-select mode lets you remove multiple items in one action with a single confirm.
Drag-and-drop reorder
Reorder expense rows by dragging — works on both touch (mobile) and mouse (desktop).
Inline amount editing
Click any amount cell to edit the value in-place without opening a modal.
Over-budget pill
Alert badge appears in the header when total expenses exceed total income for the month.
Spending heatmap calendar
Calendar grid showing daily spend intensity — red days = heavy spend, green = light. Reveals your true spending patterns.
Category drill-down
Click any category to see its itemised breakdown for the month — every expense that contributed to the total.
Category trend chart
Month-over-month spending trend per category — see if your dining spend is slowly creeping up.
Shortfall banner
Warning banner appears when projected month-end expenses will exceed income, before it actually happens.
Quick Add
Fast expense entry modal that skips the full form — name, amount, done in under 10 seconds.
Undo support
State snapshot is taken before destructive changes. One tap to restore if you delete something by accident.

Auto-archive
Automatically archive months older than a configurable threshold — keeps the active view fast and clean.
Archive viewer
Browse all archived months in expand/collapse groups — all your history, always accessible.
Archive badge
Notification count showing how many months have been auto-archived — so nothing disappears silently.
Restore from archive

Scheduled rules

11  ·  Security & Privacy
Your data stays
on your device.

PIN lock, AES-GCM encryption, no account required, session-based unlock so refresh never interrupts you — and a full reset that truly wipes everything.

PIN lock
4 or 6-digit PIN protects the app at launch. Exponential lockout after failed attempts prevents brute force.
Session-based unlock
Enter PIN once per tab session. Page refresh within your inactivity window never asks again — only a new tab or manual lock does.
Configurable auto-lock
Choose when the PIN re-asks: 30 min, 1 hr, 4 hr, 8 hr, 1 day, or never (tab-close only). Set in Settings.
AES-GCM encryption
Optional encryption of all stored data using a PIN-derived key. Data at rest is unreadable without your PIN.
Passphrase strength checker
Visual feedback on your recovery passphrase strength as you type — so you don't accidentally set a weak one.
Encrypted payload detection
Auto-detects on load whether stored data is encrypted and asks for PIN before attempting to decrypt.
No cloud by default
All data in localStorage/IndexedDB. No account required to use the app. Nothing leaves your device unless you turn on sync.
Full reset
Wipe every byte of data — requires typing the word RESET to confirm. Truly irreversible.
